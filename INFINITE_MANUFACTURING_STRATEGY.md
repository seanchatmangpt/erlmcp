## THE SUNK COST PROBLEM (Traditional SaaS)

### Datadog's Constraint
- Builds new observability feature: $5M investment
- Feature ships, doesn't sell well
- **Sunk cost fallacy kicks in**: "We spent $5M, we HAVE to make this work!"
- Double down: Add more features, hire sales team, create marketing campaign
- Total sunk cost: $10M
- **Result**: Company is now trapped by their own investment

### Their Pricing Constraint
- Must charge enough to recover $10M sunk cost
- Must sell to enough customers to amortize investment
- **Can't pivot**: Already committed $10M, can't write it off
- **Can't customize**: Would need another $5M to build custom version
