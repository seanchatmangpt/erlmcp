## Overview

The ERLMCP v3 Business Continuity Plan (BCP) is a comprehensive framework designed to ensure the uninterrupted operation of ERLMCP services during disruptive events. Built specifically for Fortune 500 requirements, this plan delivers enterprise-grade resilience with:

- **99.999% availability** for mission-critical systems
- **RTO ≤ 4 hours** and **RPO ≤ 15 minutes** for tiered services
- **Zero data loss** for production environments
- **Automated recovery** with self-healing capabilities

### Key Features

#### Comprehensive Protection
- **Multi-site Resilience**: Active-active architecture across 3+ geographically distributed sites
- **Automated Recovery**: Self-healing with automated failover capabilities
- **Real-time Monitoring**: 24/7 operations dashboard with AI-driven anomaly detection
- **Regulatory Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR readiness

#### Fortune 500 Compliance
- **Executive Oversight**: Board-level governance and reporting
- **Risk Management**: Comprehensive risk assessment and mitigation
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Proactive and transparent stakeholder engagement
- **Third-Party Validation**: Regular independent testing and validation

#### Operational Excellence
- **Automated Testing**: Continuous validation of recovery capabilities
- **Continuous Improvement**: Data-driven optimization and enhancement
- **Staff Training**: Comprehensive training programs and certification
- **Performance Metrics**: KPI-driven performance monitoring
